# Privacy Policy for Lovance

**Last Updated:** [February 19, 2026]

## Introduction

Lovance ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your name and email address (if using email authentication)
- **Authentication Data:** We use Firebase Authentication, which may collect authentication tokens and identifiers from third-party providers (Google, Apple) if you choose to sign in with those services
- **Profile Information:** Your name, anniversary date, and optional profile picture

### Content You Share
- **Photos:** Images you share with your partner
- **Notes:** Text messages you send to your partner
- **Status Updates:** Your status updates and emojis
- **Drawings:** Any drawings you create and share

### Technical Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app (for improving the service)
- **Log Data:** Error logs and diagnostic information

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Enable communication between you and your partner
- Sync data across your devices
- Improve and personalize your experience
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored
- **Firebase Firestore:** Your user profile, messages, and content are stored in Google Firebase Firestore
- **Firebase Storage:** Your photos and images are stored in Google Firebase Storage
- **Local Storage:** Some data is cached locally on your device for offline access

### Security Measures
- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
- Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (industry-standard security)
- Access to your data is restricted to you and your connected partner only
- We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure only authorized users can access data

## Data Sharing

### With Your Partner
- Your name, profile picture, and anniversary date are shared with your connected partner
- All content you send (photos, notes, status updates) is shared with your partner
- This sharing is limited to the partner you explicitly connect with using a partner code

### With Third Parties
- **Firebase (Google):** We use Firebase for authentication, database, and storage services. Firebase's privacy policy applies: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Apple Sign-In:** If you use Apple Sign-In, Apple's privacy policy applies: https://www.apple.com/privacy/
- **Google Sign-In:** If you use Google Sign-In, Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes outside the app

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Delete:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your personal information
- **Withdraw Consent:** Disconnect from your partner at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at [jonathansudarpo@gmail.com] or use the in-app settings.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** When you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days
- **Content:** Content shared with your partner may remain accessible to them until they delete it

## Children's Privacy

Lovance is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. By using Lovance, you consent to the transfer of your data to these countries.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending you a notification (for significant changes)
